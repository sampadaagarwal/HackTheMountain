Healing Minds

"Healing minds aims to give hope to every person experiencing stress, anxiety and depression."

At its core, Healing minds is an idea, a movement, and a metaphor for hope. Arising from her personal journey with anxiety and depression. The website combines knowledge and domain expertise, to create awareness about mental health, reduce stigma associated with mental illness, and provide credible mental health resources. Healing Minds program and outreach are conducted and implemented through partnerships and collaborations.
